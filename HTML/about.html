About Condor Entertainment

Who we are

Condor Entertainment was started as a means to Highlight West Lothians Brilliant and Unheard Stories, Dive deep into the History of our County and Rescue these Stories from Obscurity, this Naturally Evolved into offering Branding and Promotion to Companies in the Greater Edinburgh Area as a means to further benefit the Community and use our Talent Efficiently, with next to no outside funding Condor has managed to Utilise its Medium, Brilliant Crew and Industry Experience to Deliver Documentaries on Some of Scotlands most Fascinating Untold Stories.
