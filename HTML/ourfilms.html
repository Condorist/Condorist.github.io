The Dechmont Woods Case

A strange police case from the 70s. World War II Veteran Bob Taylor gets attacked by a strange object in the Dechmont woods of West Lothian on November the 9th 1979. The only suspected UFO incident to be investigated by the police on the British Isles and the only one in the world to have a forensic investigation. Hear from detective inspector Ian Wark, chief investigator of the case, Malcolm Robinson, expert in similar cases and Drew McAdam Scottish renowned mentalist and body language expert as we dive into what happened on that cold November morning. Premiered in Bathgate Cinema on the 11th of November 2023

Bangour District Asylum Documentary
